# Sentinel Methodology

> **Version:** 1.0  
> **Classification:** Public

---

## 1. The Sovereignty Protocol

Sentinel does not generate entries. It establishes the structural conditions under which an entry becomes valid. Its objective is to insulate the operator from the two forces that destroy capital: internal cognitive failure and external market chaos.

The independent professional does not need more signals. They need **immutable boundaries**.

---

## 2. The Two Fronts of Defence

### Internal — The Cognitive Firewall
The primary adversary in trading is not the market. It is the operator's own psychology. Overconfidence, revenge trading, checklist circumvention — these are not character flaws; they are predictable cognitive patterns that emerge under pressure.

Sentinel treats emotional states as **quantifiable predictive models**, not abstract feelings. It maps the operator into affect quadrants, measures prediction error, and applies calibrated friction before capital is committed. Discipline becomes the default operational state, not a daily struggle.

### External — The Macro Shield
Regime shifts, intermarket divergence, and scheduled high-impact events compress volatility into unpredictable bursts. Technical setups that were valid five minutes ago become invalid five minutes later.

Sentinel monitors cross-asset pulse indicators and economic calendars in real time. It does not predict the direction of the shock; it predicts the **conditions under which exposure becomes irrational** and blocks deployment before the damage occurs.

---

## 3. Core Principles

**Defence in Depth**
Layered safeguards: cognitive monitoring first, discipline enforcement second, macro filtering third. Each layer is independently sufficient to prevent catastrophic action; together they provide redundant protection.

**Friction, Not Frictionlessness**
Conventional design pursues frictionlessness. Sentinel pursues the opposite: **calibrated friction**. Every potentially destructive action must pass through an intentional checkpoint that forces the operator to acknowledge the risk.

**Non-Repudiation**
All gate decisions are logged with timestamp, trigger condition, and operator response. Accountability is enforced by architecture, not assumed.

---

## 4. What Sentinel Does NOT Do

| Claim | Reality |
|-------|---------|
| Predict price direction | No. It defines safe conditions. Direction is your inference. |
| Generate buy/sell signals | No. It highlights when acting is unsafe. Execution is your decision. |
| Guarantee profit | No. It prevents catastrophic action. Edge validation remains your responsibility. |
| Replace your judgement | Absolutely not. Sentinel is a defensive gate, not an autopilot. |

Every Sentinel release undergoes:

1. Historical cognitive stress test (simulated emotional states)
2. Macro shock test (NFP, FOMC, geopolitical events)
3. Bypass audit (automated verification of gate integrity)
4. Forward observation (3 months live market monitoring)

Results available to verified licence holders upon request.
